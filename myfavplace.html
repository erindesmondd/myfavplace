Paradise Island, The Bahamas

Paradise Island, The Bahamas is my favortie place to visit because there is something to do for everyone. There are beaches, pools, shops, and a waterpark. Everything is within walking distance and close to the hotels within Atlantis. I have been once before and I am going again in the middle of December for a gymnastics meet. Everyone in The Bahamas is so friendly and will try to help you no matter what. The Bahamas is a magical place!
